Briards

A heart wrapped in fur

The briards. or berger de brie. is a large breed of dog traditionally used as a herder and guardina of sheep.

Breed History

The Briard, which is believed to have originated in French Army as sentries, messengers and to search for wounded soldiers because of its fine sense of hearing. Briards were used in the First World War almost to the point of extinction. Currently the population of briards is slowly recovering. Charlemagne,Napoleon,Thomas Jefferson and Lafayette all owned briards.

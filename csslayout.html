intro

It is the age of technology and internet. Every day a new technology is born and it is accelerating in a very rapid speed.Proctoring is another word for exam supervision. So joining the two words ‘online’ and ‘proctoring’, it is very clear what it really means.

Objective

To create an effective online examination service for educational institutions. To make proctoring as effective and strict as in physical examinations. To create an easy-to-use and simple interface for both the examinee, examiner and proctor.

application

• Proctor uses video and audio with an intelligent movement detecting software to invigilate the exam. It continually monitors and analyzes the behaviors and activities of test takers. This app detects any other applications running in the background
